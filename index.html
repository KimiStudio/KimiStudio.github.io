About Our Studio

Welcome to KimiStudio, where we create beautiful, handcrafted fashion pieces for children. Our designs combine comfort, style, and durability to ensure your child looks and feels amazing.

We also craft elegant candles that complement our fashion line, creating a complete sensory experience for your home.

Each piece is made with attention to detail and a commitment to quality that sets us apart.
