Our Services

We offer a variety of services for your children's fashion needs. Please contact us through WeChat to discuss your requirements and create something special.

  • Candle making workshops
  • Alterations and adjustments

Booking Information:

Appointments are available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability.

A 50% deposit is required for custom design orders.
